Detailed Description

A forward regional elementary language.

A forward regional elementary language is an elementary langauge \((u, \Lambda)\) with an order over the fractional parts of \(\mathbb{T}_{0,N},\mathbb{T}_{1,N},\dots,\mathbb{T}_{N,N}\), where \(\mathbb{T}_{i,N} = \tau_{i} + \tau_{i+1} \dots \tau_{N}\).

Invariant
elementary.wordSize() + 1 == fractionalOrder.size()
