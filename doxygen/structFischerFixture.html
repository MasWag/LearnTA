Detailed Description

Fixture of the Fischer benchmark, originally from [AL'94].

Author
Masaki Waga
Date
2023/01/25.

We use a variant of the Fischer benchmark in https://github.com/ticktac-project/benchmarks/tree/master/fischer/tchecker. We focus on the Fischer protocol with three processes.

  • [AL'94]: Martin Abadi and Leslie Lamport, An Old-Fashioned Recipe for Real Time, ACM Transactions on Programming Languages and Systems, 16(5), pp. 1543-1571, 1994.
