Detailed Description

Fixture of the FDDI benchmark, originally from [DOTY'96].

Author
Masaki Waga
Date
2023/01/24.

We use a variant of the FDDI benchmark in https://github.com/ticktac-project/benchmarks/tree/master/fddi/tchecker. We focus on the FDDI benchmark with two clients.

  • [DOTY'96]: C. Daws, A. Oliveiro, S. Tripakis, and S. Yovine. The tool Kronos, Hybrid Systems III, 1996.

Member Data Documentation

◆ alphabet

const std::vector<learnta::Alphabet> FDDIFixture::alphabet = {'a', 'b', 'c', 'd', 'e', 'f'}

The mapping of the alphabet from the original benchmark is as follows

  • P1@TT, R@TT1: a
  • P1@RT, R@RT1: b
  • P2@TT, R@TT2: c
  • P2@RT, R@RT2: d
  • P1@tau: e
  • P2@tau: f
